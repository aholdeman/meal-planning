# BBQ Meatballs

## Ingredients
### Meatballs
- 1 lb ground beef
- 1 egg, whisked
- 2 tbsp whole milk
- 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
- 1/4 cup breadcrumbs (or quick oatmeal)
- 1 tsp sea salt
- 1/4 tsp black pepper
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1/2 tsp onion powder
- 1/4 cup parmesan cheese

### Sauce
- 1.5 cups BBQ sauce (12 oz)
- 8 oz crushed pineapple
- 1/2 cup red onion, diced
- 1 green bell pepper, diced
- 1 tsp minced garlic
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 1/4 tsp pepper

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
2. Combine beef, egg, milk, Worcestershire in a bowl.
3. Add breadcrumbs, parmesan, salt, pepper, garlic powder, onion powder; mix.
4. Scoop 1-tbsp portions, roll into balls.
5. Bake 12-15 min until internal temp reaches 165°F.
6. Combine BBQ sauce, pineapple, onion, pepper, garlic, salt, pepper in a slow cooker.
7. Add cooked meatballs, coat with sauce.
8. Cook on low 4-5 hr or high 2-3 hr (adjust down if using frozen meatballs).
9. Last 30 min, ajar the lid to thicken sauce.
10. Serve over rice or noodles.
