# Classic Shepherd's Pie

## Ingredients
### Meat Filling
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 cup chopped yellow onion
- 1 lb 90% lean ground beef (or ground lamb)
- 2 tsp dried parsley
- 1 tsp dried rosemary
- 1 tsp dried thyme
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp black pepper
- 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
- 2 tbsp tomato paste
- 1 cup beef broth
- 1 cup frozen peas & carrots
- 1/2 cup frozen corn

### Potato Topping
- 1.5-2 lbs russet potatoes, peeled and cubed
- 8 tbsp unsalted butter
- 1/3 cup half & half
- 1/2 tsp garlic powder
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/4 tsp black pepper
- 1/4 cup parmesan cheese

## Instructions
1. Heat oil in a large skillet, cook onions 5 min.
2. Add beef, parsley, rosemary, thyme, salt, pepper; brown 6-8 min.
3. Add Worcestershire and garlic, cook 1 min.
4. Stir in flour and tomato paste until incorporated.
5. Add broth, peas & carrots, corn. Boil, then simmer 5 min. Set aside; preheat oven to 400°F.
6. Boil potatoes until fork-tender, 10-15 min. Drain, return to pot.
7. Mash with butter, half & half, garlic powder, salt, pepper. Stir in parmesan.
8. Spread meat mixture in a 9x9 or 7x11 baking dish. Top with mashed potatoes.
9. Bake uncovered at 400°F for 25-30 min. Cool 15 min before serving.
