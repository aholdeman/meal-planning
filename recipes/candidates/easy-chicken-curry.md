# Easy Chicken Curry

## Ingredients
- 1.5 tbsp oil (vegetable or canola)
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 2 tsp ginger, grated
- 1/2 onion, finely chopped
- 1 lb chicken thighs, sliced
- 2.5 tbsp curry powder
- 1.25 cups coconut milk, full fat
- 1.5 cups chicken stock, low sodium
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 3/4 cup frozen peas
- 1/4 cup cilantro, chopped

## Instructions
1. Heat oil in a skillet over medium-high. Cook garlic, ginger, and onion 3 min until onion is translucent.
2. Add chicken, cook until no longer pink.
3. Add curry powder, cook 2 min.
4. Add coconut milk and chicken stock. Lower heat to medium, simmer rapidly 10 min until sauce thickens.
5. Add peas and salt, cook 2 more min. Taste and adjust. Garnish with cilantro.
6. Serve over rice, noodles, or mashed potato.
