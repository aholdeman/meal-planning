# Zuppa Toscana Soup

## Ingredients
- 1 lb spicy Italian ground sausage (mild for kid-friendly)
- 4 tbsp butter
- 1/2 white onion, diced
- 1 tbsp minced garlic
- 6 cups chicken broth
- 2 cups water
- 4-5 yellow potatoes, cut into 1-inch pieces
- 3 tsp salt, or to taste
- 1 tsp black pepper
- 2 cups heavy cream
- 4 cups chopped kale
- chopped bacon or bacon bits and grated parmesan, for topping (optional)

## Instructions
1. Sauté sausage in a large pot 5-6 min until browned. Remove with a slotted spoon, set aside.
2. In the same pot, melt butter and sauté onion until translucent. Add garlic, sauté 1 min.
3. Add broth, water, potatoes, salt, and pepper. Boil until potatoes are tender.
4. Stir in kale and heavy cream. Return sausage to the pot.
5. Taste, adjust seasoning. Serve topped with parmesan and/or bacon.
