# Soy Ginger Salmon

## Ingredients
- 1.5 lb salmon, cut into 4 portions
- 1/4 cup soy sauce
- 1 tbsp rice wine vinegar
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tbsp honey
- 4 inches fresh ginger, grated
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1/2 lime, juiced
- 1-2 scallions, sliced
- 1/2 tsp sesame seeds

## Instructions
1. Whisk soy sauce, vinegar, olive oil, ginger, garlic, honey, and lime juice.
2. Place salmon skin-side down in a baking dish, pour sauce over. Bake at 400°F for 15-20 min until internal temp reaches 145°F.
3. Spoon sauce over salmon, garnish with sesame seeds and scallions.
