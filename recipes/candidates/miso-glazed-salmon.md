# Miso-Glazed Salmon

## Ingredients
- 4 salmon fillets
- 3 tbsp white miso paste
- 2 tbsp mirin
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp brown sugar
- 2 scallions, sliced (for garnish)
- sesame seeds (for garnish)

## Instructions
1. Whisk miso, mirin, soy sauce, and brown sugar into a glaze.
2. Coat salmon fillets, marinate 15-30 min.
3. Broil or pan-sear until glaze caramelizes and salmon is just cooked through (~8-10 min).
4. Garnish with scallions and sesame seeds.
