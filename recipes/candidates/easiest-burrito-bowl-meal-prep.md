# Easiest Burrito Bowl Meal Prep

## Ingredients
- 4 cups cooked rice (about 1.5 cups uncooked rice + 2.5 cups water)
- 1 lb ground turkey or beef
- Taco seasoning, to taste (about 2-3 tbsp)
- 1/2 cup water (for simmering the meat)
- 1 cup frozen corn kernels
- 1 (15 oz) can black beans, rinsed and drained
- 4 oz shredded cheese (about 1 cup)
- 1 1/3 cups salsa
- 2 green onions, sliced

## Instructions
1. Cook rice according to package directions.
2. While rice cooks, brown the ground meat in a skillet over medium heat until fully cooked. Add taco seasoning and 1/2 cup water; simmer about 5 min until reduced to a saucy consistency.
3. Thaw the corn, rinse and drain the beans, shred the cheese, and slice the green onions.
4. Divide rice among four containers, then distribute the meat, corn, beans, cheese, salsa, and green onions equally among them.
5. Reheat 1-2 min before eating to melt the cheese and warm everything through.

## Notes
Found via web search for a quick, fridge-friendly weekly lunch batch -- explicitly holds 3-4 days refrigerated, which covers Tue/Wed/Thu after a Monday-night cook. Recipe calls for ground turkey or beef interchangeably; pick whichever's on hand.
