# Baked Ziti

## Ingredients
### Meat sauce
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 2 large onions, finely diced
- 8 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 lb ground beef
- 48 oz marinara sauce
- salt, pepper, Italian seasoning to taste

### Pasta & assembly
- 1 lb dry ziti pasta
- 12 slices provolone cheese
- 8 oz mozzarella, grated
- 8 oz sour cream
- 4 oz parmesan, grated

## Instructions
1. Cook onions in olive oil until translucent. Add garlic, cook 30 sec.
2. Add ground beef, brown thoroughly.
3. Stir in marinara, salt, pepper, Italian seasoning. Simmer 20-30 min.
4. Cook ziti al dente, drain.
5. In a 9x13 dish, layer half the pasta, half the provolone, half the mozzarella.
6. Spread half the meat sauce, dollop all the sour cream, smooth.
7. Layer remaining pasta, provolone, mozzarella.
8. Top with remaining meat sauce, finish with parmesan.
9. Bake uncovered at 350°F for ~30 min until bubbly.

## Notes
Recipe is explicitly freezer-friendly: cool completely, wrap in foil + plastic. Bake from frozen at 350°F for 1 hour.
