# One Pan Greek Meatballs with Lemon Feta Orzo

## Ingredients
### Meatballs
- 1 lb ground turkey
- 1 large egg
- 1 cup fresh breadcrumbs
- 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
- 1/4 cup diced onion
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 2 tsp dried oregano
- 1 tsp dried parsley
- 1/4 tsp sea salt
- 1/4 tsp pepper
- 1 tbsp olive oil (for cooking)

### Lemon Feta Orzo
- 1 cup dry orzo
- 2 1/4 cups chicken stock
- 1/4 cup diced onion
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- 1 tbsp lemon zest
- 2 tbsp butter
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- 3 garlic cloves, minced
- 1/4 tsp salt
- feta cheese, for topping

## Instructions
1. Mix turkey, egg, breadcrumbs, onion, herbs, salt, feta, and garlic. Roll into 1.5-2 tbsp balls.
2. Preheat oven to 400°F.
3. Heat olive oil in a skillet, cook meatballs 3-4 min per side until golden. Remove and set aside.
4. Add butter, onion, and orzo to the skillet. Cook 2 min.
5. Stir in garlic, oregano, salt. Cook 30 sec more.
6. Pour in chicken stock, lemon juice, and zest. Bring to a boil, stir well.
7. Return meatballs to skillet, transfer to oven for 15 min.
8. Confirm meatballs are fully cooked. Top with feta before serving.
