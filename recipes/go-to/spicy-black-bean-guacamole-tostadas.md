# Spicy Black Bean and Guacamole Tostadas

## Ingredients
### Tostadas
- 6-8 corn tortillas
- 1/2 cup cheese
- Oil, for crisping
- Cilantro, for garnish
- Hot sauce, to taste

### Black Bean Puree
- 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
- 1/2 onion, roughly chopped
- 2 garlic cloves
- 2 chipotles in adobo
- 1 tbsp adobo sauce
- 1/2 tsp cumin
- 1/2 tsp salt
- Black pepper, freshly cracked
- Splash of water

### Guacamole
- 1 avocado
- 1 lime, juiced
- 2-3 tbsp onion, finely chopped
- 1/4 tsp salt, plus more to taste

## Instructions
1. Roughly chop the onion and garlic; sauté in oil over medium heat 5-7 min.
2. Add drained/rinsed black beans, chipotles, adobo sauce, cumin, salt, pepper, and a splash of water. Simmer until heated through.
3. Blend the bean mixture until smooth; adjust salt to taste.
4. For the guacamole: finely chop onion and mash with a fork until translucent, then combine with avocado, salt, and lime juice; adjust seasoning.
5. Add a thin layer of oil to a skillet over medium heat. Layer beans and cheese on tortillas, cook 1-2 min until brown spots form.
6. Top with guacamole, cilantro, hot sauce, and desired toppings. Serve immediately.

## Notes
Recipe yields 2 servings -- double (or more) for the whole household. Crisps tostadas with a thin oil layer in a skillet rather than deep frying; can also bake your own tostada shells instead.
