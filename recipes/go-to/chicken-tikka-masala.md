# Chicken Tikka Masala

## Ingredients
- 1.5 lb chicken thighs, cubed
- 1 cup plain yogurt
- 2 tbsp tikka masala spice blend
- 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes
- 1 cup heavy cream
- 1 onion, diced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp ginger, minced
- salt, pepper, oil, garam masala

## Instructions
1. Marinate chicken in yogurt + spices, 30 min minimum (overnight is better).
2. Sear chicken in batches, set aside.
3. Saute onion, garlic, ginger until soft. Add tomatoes, simmer 10 min.
4. Stir in cream, return chicken to pot, simmer until cooked through.
5. Finish with garam masala, serve over rice.

## Notes
Freezes well in portioned containers — sauce holds up better than the rice, so freeze rice separately if doing a big batch.
